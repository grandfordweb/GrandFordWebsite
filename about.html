About us


Grandford Associates Consultants Limited was established in 2008 and is a member of Professional Insurance Brokers Association. We are an independent broker with access to all the major insurers, including insurers who provide niche covers for niche risks. Since our formation, we have built up a reputation for professionalism and excellence, and pride ourselves on providing consistently good value for our clients, whether business or personal.

We look to provide comprehensive and cost-effective services and contribute to the success and profitability of our clients. In the event of a claim, we will be there to make sure your needs are looked after and is professionally handled .

Our independence guarantees that we represent you and your interests at all times. If you are looking for an insurance broker with Integrity, Expertise and Ability , we are here to serve you.
